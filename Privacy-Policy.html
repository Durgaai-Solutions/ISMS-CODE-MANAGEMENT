1 Who We Are

Durgaai Solutions Is The Developer Of The ISMS Parents Portal Android application ("App"). The App Is A Component Of The Integrated School Management System (ISMS), An ERP Platform Used By Registered Schools To Manage Their Day-To-Day Academic And Administrative Operations.

The App Serves As A Read-Only Portal Allowing Parents And Guardians To View Their Child's School-Related Data, Including Fees, Attendance, Exam Results, Notices, And More.

2 Information We Collect

The App Retrieves Data From Your School's Private Backend Server After You Successfully Log In. We Do Not Collect Any Data Independently Of Your School's System. All Information Displayed In The App Originates From Data Entered By Your School's Administrators.

3 How We Use Your Information

All Data Retrieved From The School's Server Is Used Solely To Display Information To The Logged-In Parent Or Guardian Within The App. Specifically, We Use Your Data To:

  • Authenticate Your Account And Maintain A Secure Session
  • Display Your Child's Academic And Administrative Records (Fees, Attendance, Results, Etc.)
  • Show Unread School Notices And Announcements
  • Allow You To Confirm Or View Gate Pass Requests
  • Display Your Child's Timetable, Library Status, Transport Booking, And Medical Records

We Do Not Use Your Data For Advertising, Profiling, Marketing, Or Any Purpose Beyond The Above Direct Functionality.

4 Data Storage & Security

On Your Device: The App Stores Your Session Token, School Code, API URL, And A Cached Copy Of Your Student Profile In Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256-GCM / AES-256-SIV Encryption). This Data Persists Only While You Are Logged In And Is Permanently Deleted On Logout.

On The School Server: All Student Data Resides Exclusively On The School's Private Backend Server (Hosted By The School Or Durgaai Solutions On Their Behalf). Communication Between The App And The Server Is Protected Via HTTPS (TLS).

No Cloud Storage By Durgaai Solutions: Durgaai Solutions Does Not Maintain A Central Database Of Student Or Parent Data. We Have No Access To Individual School Data Unless The School Explicitly Grants Us Administrative Access For Support Purposes.

Session Tokens A Unique Session Token Is Issued On Each Login And Stored Encrypted On Your Device. It Is Invalidated On Logout Or When You Log In From Another Device.

5 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We Do Not Sell, Rent, Trade, Or Share Your Personal Information With Any Third Party, Except In These Limited Circumstances:

  • Your School: Data Is Exchanged Only With The Backend Server Of The School You Selected — You Chose This Connection By Entering Your School Code.
  • Legal Requirements: We May Disclose Information If Required By Applicable Law, Court Order, Or Governmental Authority.
  • Safety: We May Disclose Information If We Believe In Good Faith That Disclosure Is Necessary To Protect The Rights, Property, Or Safety Of Durgaai Solutions, Our Users, Or The Public.

There Are No Advertising Networks, No Analytics Providers, And No Social Media Integrations In This App That Receive Any User Data.

6 Data Retention

Data Cached On Your Device (Student Profile, Session Token, School Code) Is Retained Until You Log Out Of The App, At Which Point All Locally Stored Data Is Permanently Erased From The Device.

Data On The School's Backend Server Is Managed And Retained By The School Administration According To Their Own Policies. Contact Your School For Information About How Long Records Are Kept.

If You Uninstall The App Without Logging Out First, Android Will Automatically Delete All App Data From The Device.

7 Children's Privacy

This App Is Designed For Parents And Guardians Of School-Enrolled Students, Not For Children To Use Directly. The App Displays Children's Academic Records Strictly To Their Parent Or Guardian Who Has Been Issued Credentials By The School.

We Do Not Knowingly Collect Personal Information Directly From Children. All Student Data Is Provided To The App By The School Administration On Behalf Of Enrolled Families.

Credential Security Parent Login Credentials Are Issued By Your School's Administration. Keep Your Username And Password Confidential. Do Not Share Them With Your Child Or Anyone Who Should Not Have Access To The Student's Records.

8 Your Rights

Since All Personal Data Originates From And Is Managed By Your School's System, Most Data Rights (Access, Correction, Deletion) Are Exercised Through Your School's Administration. However, You Have The Following Rights With Respect To Data On Your Device:

  • Right to Access: All Data The App Has Stored On Your Device Is Visible Within The App Itself.
  • Right to Delete (Device Data): Log Out Of The App To Permanently Erase All Data From Your Device. Uninstalling The App Also Removes All Locally Stored Data.
  • Right to Request Account Deletion: Contact Your School Administration To Request Deletion Of Your Parent Portal Credentials And Associated Student Records From The School's System.
  • Correction: If Any Student Data Shown In The App Is Incorrect, Contact Your School's Administrative Office To Have It Corrected At The Source.

9 Third-Party Services

The App Uses The Following Limited Set Of Third-Party Libraries. None Of Them Receive Personally Identifiable Information From Users Of This App:

  • Retrofit 2 / OkHttp3 (Square): HTTP Networking Library Used To Communicate With The School's Backend API Over HTTPS. No Data Is Sent To Square's Servers.
  • Gson (Google): JSON Parsing Library. Processes Data In Memory Only; No Data Transmitted Externally.
  • Glide (bumptech): Image Loading Library For Displaying Student Photos. Images Are Loaded Directly From Your School's Server.
  • Material Components For Android (Google): UI Component Library. No Data Collection.
  • AndroidX Jetpack Libraries (Google): Architecture Components (ViewModel, LiveData, Navigation, EncryptedSharedPreferences). No External Data Transmission.

The App Does Not Integrate Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, AdMob, Facebook SDK, Or Any Similar Data-Collecting Third-Party SDK.
