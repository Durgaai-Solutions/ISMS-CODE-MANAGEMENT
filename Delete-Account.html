1
About This Page

In Compliance With The Google Play Developer Policy, All Users Of The ISMS Parents Portal Have The Right To Request Permanent Deletion Of Their Account And All Associated Personal Data Held Within The Application And Its Backend Systems.

This Page Provides A Simple, Secure Form To Submit Your Deletion Request Directly To Our Support Team. All Requests Are Reviewed And Processed Within 30 Business Days Of Verified Submission.

  • Applicable To All Registered Parent And Guardian Accounts
  • Covers All Personal Data Collected During App Registration And Usage
  • A Confirmation Email Is Sent To Your Registered Address Upon Completion
  • No App Login Is Required To Submit This Form

2
How To Request Deletion

Follow These Three Steps To Submit Your Account Deletion Request:

1

Fill Out The Request Form

Provide Your Registered Name, Email Address, Mobile Number, Student Name, And School Code To Uniquely Identify Your Account. Select Your Reason For Deletion.

2

Confirm & Submit

Check The Confirmation Checkbox Acknowledging That Account Deletion Is Permanent And Irreversible, Then Submit The Form. You Will See An On-Screen Confirmation.

3

Verification & Processing

Our Support Team Verifies Your Identity Using The Details Provided And Processes The Deletion. A Confirmation Email Is Sent To Your Registered Address Once Complete.

3
Data Deletion Policy

The Table Below Explains What Data Will Be Permanently Deleted And What May Be Retained Under Legal Or Institutional Obligations:

Data Category Action Details
Account Credentials Permanently Deleted Username, Hashed Password, And All Active Session Tokens Are Fully Removed.
Personal Contact Info Permanently Deleted Registered Name, Email Address, And Mobile Number Are Erased From Our Systems.
Push Notification Tokens Permanently Deleted All Device Tokens Are Removed. No Further Notifications Will Be Sent To Your Device.
App Usage & Activity Logs Permanently Deleted Login History And In-App Activity Records Associated With Your Account Are Removed.
Student Academic Records May Be Retained Marks, Attendance, And Progress Reports May Be Retained If Required By The School Administration Or Applicable Educational Regulations.
Fee & Financial Records May Be Retained Transaction And Invoice Records May Be Retained As Required By Financial Or Tax Compliance Regulations.
Processing Timeframe Deletion Requests Are Processed Within Up To 30 Business Days Of Verified Submission. A Confirmation Email Will Be Sent To Your Registered Address Once Your Account Has Been Permanently Deleted.
