01 About Durgaai Solutions

Durgaai Solutions Is India's Leading EdTech Infrastructure Provider. We Are Devoted To Digitally Catapulting Traditional Educational Campuses Into Highly-Efficient, Automated Environments By Providing State-of-the-Art Software Systems That Streamline The Entire Lifecycle Of Student And Administrator Handling.

02 The Core Philosophy

We Realized That Standard "Off-the-Shelf" School Systems Often Caused More Pain Than They Solved Due To Bloated UIs And Slow Servers. We Created the ISMS Core Specifically Around Robust SQL Database Mappings, Native Android App Interfaces, And a High-Speed Web Portal. The Result Is A Unified Platform Where Teachers Submit Data Effortlessly, Parents Remain Instantly Informed, And Admins Wield Total Operational Control.

03 Technology & Mission

We Refuse To Deploy On Generic Shared Hosting. We Proudly Provision Custom Dedicated Servers For Our Indian Schools To Ensure Blazing-Fast Invoice Generation, Error-Free Exam Calculations, And Immediate Push Notifications. Our Mission Is To Handle The Complex Backend IT So Educators Can Focus Entirely On Teaching.
